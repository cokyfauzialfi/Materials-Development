Systematic Evaluation Algorithm

Professional Workflow for ELT Materials in Indonesia

Step 01

Clarify Purpose & Context

Define why you are evaluating (Selection vs Review) and identify constraints (e.g., Rural Pesantren vs Urban School).

Step 02

Select Evaluation Framework

Choose McDonough & Shaw for rapid screening or Cunningsworth for deep analysis.

Step 03 & 04

Develop Checklist & Examine

Create 30-50 items (Skills, Method, Culture) and systematically score the material.

Decision

Does it meet core Indonesian criteria & curriculum?

Step 05

Analyze & Recommend

Make evidence-based recommendations: Adopt, Reject, or Adapt.

Step 06

Document Process

Create an institutional resource with criteria and evidence of judgment.

Step 07

Implement & Continuous Cycle

Ongoing whilst-use monitoring and post-use impact assessment.

Criteria Not Met

The material failed the decision gate. Algorithm requires you to return to Step 2 to screen alternative candidates.
