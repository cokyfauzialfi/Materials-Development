"Match the Technique" Challenge

Identify: Reduction, Addition, Modification, Simplification, or Reorganization

A. Changing a dialogue about "The New York Subway" to "The TransJakarta Busway."
B. Removing a reading passage about "Wine Tasting" in a conservative school.
C. Breaking a 30-word complex sentence into three shorter, 10-word sentences.

Key: A: Modification, B: Reduction, C: Simplification

Scenario-Based Application

SCENARIO:

You are teaching in a rural area. The textbook requires a "YouTube video and blog comments." Your school has no internet and limited smartphones.

1. Phase 1 (Analysis): Identify the mismatch.

2. Phase 2 (Planning): Plan your solution.

Sample Response:

  • Analysis: Practical feasibility mismatch (infrastructure).
  • Plan: Modification. Convert video to a live roleplay and blog to a paper gallery walk.
